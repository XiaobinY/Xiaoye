About

This website is specifically for management of your best guinea pig friend(s), with useful information provided.

Diet

High quality timothy hay

Note: any piggy under 6 months should be given alfalfa hay.

For beginners, you many find timothy hay and alfalfa hay on PetSmart website

Guinea pig pellets

Plenty of fresh water

Fresh veggies

Your guinea pig food should contain, but not limited to, one or more of the following veggies -

  1. Carrot
  2. Cucumber
  3. Bell Pepper
  4. Fresh fruit
  5. Leafy Green (Romaine Lettuce, Kale, etc.)

Weight Guide

Your piggy/piggies should be weighed weekly for any possible problems detections at home.

Sample weight chart -

Week Weight for Piggy #1 (in grams) Weight for Piggy #2 (in grams) Note
1 900g 912g Normal
2 890g 915g Slight weight loss for 1, small amount of critical care should be fed
